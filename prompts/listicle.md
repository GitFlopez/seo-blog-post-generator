# Prompt: Listicle Format Article

Numbered-tips format — optimized for featured snippets and social sharing. Best for "X ways to..." and "X best..." topics.

---

## Prompt

You are an expert SEO content writer specializing in listicle-format articles that rank for featured snippets and drive social shares.

**Brief:**
- Topic: [YOUR TOPIC, e.g., "ways to reduce email overload at work"]
- Primary Keyword: [e.g., "reduce email overload"]
- Number of Items: [7 / 10 / 15 — choose one]
- Target Audience: [e.g., "knowledge workers, managers, people who feel buried in inbox"]
- Brand Tone: [e.g., "direct and practical, no fluff, respect the reader's time"]
- CTA Goal: [e.g., "download our email management template"]

**Article Structure:**

### SEO Elements
- Provide: SEO title (55-60 chars), meta description (150-160 chars), H1

### Introduction (100-150 words)
Open with the problem (how bad email overload actually is, with a concrete stat if possible). State exactly what the reader will get from this list. Keep it tight — listicle readers scan, not read.

### The List Items
For each numbered item:

**[NUMBER]. [H2 Header — Active, Specific, Keyword-Aware]**

[150-200 word explanation]
- What: briefly describe the tactic
- Why it works: one-sentence mechanism
- How to start: one concrete first action (make it specific enough to do today)

> **Quick Win:** [One-sentence version of this tip for skim-readers and social sharing]

---

After every 3rd item, add a natural transition sentence that encourages the reader to continue.

### Summary Table (Featured Snippet Bait)
After all items, add a markdown table:
| # | Tactic | Time to Implement | Difficulty |
|---|--------|------------------|------------|
| 1 | ... | ... | Easy/Medium/Hard |
...

### Conclusion (100-150 words)
Pick the 3 most important items from the list and restate them as takeaways. End with CTA aligned to the CTA Goal.

### Social Sharing Hooks
After writing the article, provide 3 tweet-length quotes (under 280 chars) pulled from the best items — ready to use as social promotion.

Write the full article now.
