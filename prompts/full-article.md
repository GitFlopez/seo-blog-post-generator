## Prompt

You are an expert SEO content writer. Write a complete, publish-ready blog article based on the brief below.

**Brief:**
- Topic/Working Title: [YOUR TOPIC, e.g., "How to Use AI Tools for Small Business Marketing"]
- Primary Keyword: [e.g., "AI tools for small business"]
- Secondary Keywords (LSI): [e.g., "AI marketing tools, small business automation, ChatGPT for business"]
- Target Audience: [e.g., "small business owners with 1-10 employees, basic tech comfort"]
- Brand Tone: [e.g., "friendly and practical, like advice from a knowledgeable friend, not corporate"]
- CTA Goal: [e.g., "sign up for our free AI tools checklist"]
- Target Word Count: [1,500-2,000 words / or specify]
- Competing Article to Beat: [optional URL or leave blank]

**Requirements:**

1. **SEO Title Options** — provide 2 options:
   - Option A: Primary keyword near front, 55-60 characters
   - Option B: Question format with keyword, 55-60 characters

2. **Meta Description** — 150-160 characters, include primary keyword, end with a soft CTA

3. **Article Structure:**
   - **H1:** Use Option A or B title (your recommendation)
   - **Introduction (150-200 words):** Open with a relatable pain point or surprising stat. Include primary keyword in first 100 words. Preview the value the article delivers.
   - **5-7 H2 Sections (200-300 words each):** Each section covers one distinct aspect of the topic. Include primary or secondary keyword naturally 1-2 times per section. Use H3 subpoints where lists or comparisons help readability.
   - **Conclusion (150-200 words):** Summarize 3 key takeaways, include a clear CTA aligned with the CTA Goal above.

4. **Internal Linking Placeholders:** Add 3-5 `[INTERNAL LINK: topic]` markers where the article should link to related content on the site.

5. **Keyword Density:** Primary keyword 1.0-1.5% (flag count at end). Secondary keywords 2-3 appearances each.

6. **Formatting:**
   - Use bold for key terms on first mention
   - Include at least one bulleted or numbered list
   - Keep paragraphs under 4 sentences for readability

Write the full article now. After the article, output a brief SEO audit line: keyword count, estimated read time, and top SERP feature this could target (featured snippet / PAA / listicle).
