### Short Description (150 chars)

### Full Description

Every business needs consistent blog content. Most don't publish because writing is slow, expensive, and inconsistent.

**SEO Blog Post Generator** gives you four battle-tested prompt templates that turn a 5-bullet brief into publish-ready content in under 2 minutes:

- **Full Article** — 1,500-2,000 words, H2/H3 structure, meta description, internal linking placeholders, keyword density audit
- **Content Brief + Outline** — 10 H2 sections, 3 title variations, SERP feature strategy, keyword placement map
- **Listicle Format** — numbered-tips article with summary table, social sharing hooks, featured snippet targeting
- **PAA Content Blocks** — FAQ answers optimized for People Also Ask extraction + copy-paste JSON-LD FAQ schema

Each prompt is tested and refined. Fill in the brackets, paste into Claude, get a draft worth publishing.

**What this costs:** $29 once.

Includes a complete worked example on "AI tools for small business" — all 4 formats so you know exactly what to expect.
